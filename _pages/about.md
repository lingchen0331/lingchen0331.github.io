Welcome! I am a second-year CS Ph.D. student at Emory University in Atlanta, GA., where I work with [Prof. Liang Zhao](http://cs.emory.edu/~lzhao41/). Before that, I earned my M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Delaware and my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Vermont.  

My research interests lie in two streams:  
- **Data Mining on Graphs**: Deep Graph Generation, Heterogeneous/Spatiotemporal Graph Representation Learning, Graph Inverse Problem
- **Social Network Analysis**: Time Series Analysis, Social Event Prediction
